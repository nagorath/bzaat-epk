BIO

Bzaat are a Psychogagi duo based in Tel Aviv Israel, Formed in 2019 and since their formation released their debut album which gained them massive and devoted followers in Israel. With hundreds of shows behind them in their home country supporting their debut album, Including tens of headlining shows and performances on the main stages of Israel’s top festivals.

When covid-19 took the music industry to a complete shutdown, Hadar and Skippy locked themself in the studio for five intensive months and written their sophomore album “Funkoholics”.
The album combines everything that Bzaat believes in: Chaos, Heaviness, and Groove. And throughout 52 minutes of music keeps the listener on his toes with intense and uncompromising virtuous playing.

Bzaat are now searching to extend their journey outside the boundaries of Israel and looking for partners in booking and management agencies, And as well a record label to release their new album with.

BZAAT Are: Hadar Levi - Guitars Aviv Bart - Drums
